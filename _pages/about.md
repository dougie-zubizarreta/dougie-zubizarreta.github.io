## About me!

I am a PhD candidate in Social Epidemiology at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. 

My work focuses on quantitative methods for intersectionality research (including I-MAIHDA) and examining structural determinants of population health and health inequities across the life-course.

Outside of academia, I love to run (long distance, trails), cook vegan food, and read non-fiction.
