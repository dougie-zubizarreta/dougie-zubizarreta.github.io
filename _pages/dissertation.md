## Title: Implementing and extending Intersectional Multilevel Analysis of Individual Heterogeneity and Discriminatory Accuracy (I-MAIHDA) to examine intersectional sleep health inequities across the life-course

### Committee: S. Bryn Austin (Chair), Jarvis T. Chen, Brent A. Coull

**Description:**

* **Aim 1:** Using data from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, implement a standard I-MAIHDA approach to describe the patterning and magnitude of sleep duration inequities across intersectional groups 
  
* **Aim 2:** Using data from the Adolescent Brain Cognitive Development Study, implement a cross-classified I-MAIHDA approach to describe the patterning and magnitude of sleep duration inequities across intersectional groups over time during adolescence

* **Aim 3:** Using data from the Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System, implement a cross-classified I-MAIHDA approach to examine whether the magnitude of sleep duration inequities across intersectional groups vary based on levels of state-level structural cisheteropatriarchy
